Chameleon Origami

  • Chameleons actually change their colour due to a number of different factors, including their mood, changes in light or temperature, or the humidity of their environment.
  • They can move their eyes separately, with each eye having a field of 180 degrees. This means they can see 360 degrees if they need to!

Cicada Origami

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus. (Females make a much quieter sound by flicking their wings.)
